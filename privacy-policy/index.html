Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 27, 2025

At SnakePP, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This document outlines what information we collect, how it is used, and the choices you have.

1. Data Collection

SnakePP does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The application is fully offline and does not access or process information such as:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, address)
  • Device data (IMEI, IP address, location)
  • Usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • Contacts, photos, media, or files
  • Camera or microphone access

2. Permissions

SnakePP does not request or require any runtime permissions from the device. The app functions locally on the user’s device and does not rely on network communication or third-party APIs.

3. Third-Party Services

This application does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, ad networks, or cloud storage platforms. All functionality is self-contained and does not communicate with external servers.

4. Children’s Privacy

SnakePP is safe for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13. The app does not contain any violent content, inappropriate language, or access to online content. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

5. Offline Functionality

The game is designed to work 100% offline. No internet connection is required at any time. The user experience is entirely local, ensuring high privacy and low resource usage.

6. Data Security

Since SnakePP does not handle any data, there are no risks associated with breaches, leaks, or unauthorized access. However, we recommend keeping your device secure and up-to-date to ensure optimal protection.

7. Policy Updates

If there are any future updates to SnakePP that may introduce new functionalities requiring data or permissions, this policy will be updated accordingly. Users will always have access to the latest version of this document through our public repository.
